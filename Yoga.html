FLow with Us

Coach Coco began her journey in the heart of Nairobi, where she discovered yoga as a path to healing and self-discovery. Now, she's here to share that magic with you. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned yogi,
Coco's classes are a sanctuary for your body and soul, designed to help you unfurl your spirit and dance through life's chaos with fearless grace. Join her, and let your flow begin.

Yoga Barre

Yoga is more than a practice—it's a journey to wholeness. Here, we offer a kaleidoscope of yoga styles to nourish every part of you:

Hatha Yoga

A foundational practice focusing on basic poses and breathwork to build strength and calm. Suitable for beginners and all.

Kundalini Yoga

Awaken your energy - Through breath, meditation, and poses, Kundalini taps into your inner power, sparking creativity and spiritual growth. It's a cosmic journey within.

Power Yoga

Ignite your strength - A vigorous, athletic style that builds muscle and stamina, Power Yoga is for those ready to challenge their limits and glow from the inside out.

 

Yoga heals, replenishes your energy, calms your mind, and reconnects you to your true self. Whether you're seeking physical strength, mental clarity, or a spiritual spark, yoga is the key to unlocking your fullest potential.
At CocoFlow, we're here to guide you every step of the way.

Our community is a vibrant tribe of seekers, dreamers, and rebels, all united by a love for movement and mindfulness.
Here, you wll find a sanctuary where every breath connects you to something bigger, where every pose is a step toward collective growth.
Whether you are flowing in our studio or joining from afar, you're never alone.
